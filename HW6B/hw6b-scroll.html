Year 2005

Between 2005 and 2021, land in Big Sky, Montana, shifted from a primarily checkerboarded, rural landscape into a highly developed, high-end resort destination. This transformation involved significant land consolidation, intense infrastructure development, and a shift toward massive private development projects.

Year 2011

By 2011, Big Sky was in a transition period. Some development had expanded compared to 2005, but the landscape had not yet reached the level of resort growth visible in 2021. This middle image helps show the gradual shift from scattered development toward more concentrated infrastructure and private property growth.

Year 2021

The 2021 satellite image was captured during snowfall, which may obscure some of the land changes, but the overall trend of increased development and land consolidation is still evident. The land development highlights increasing tourism and real estate growth in Big Sky, Montana, which has transformed the area into a popular destination for outdoor recreation and luxury living.
