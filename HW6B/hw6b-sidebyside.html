Land Cover Images Side by Side

Between 2005 and 2021, land in Big Sky, Montana, shifted from a primarily checkerboarded, rural landscape into a highly developed, high-end resort destination. This transformation involved significant land consolidation, intense infrastructure development, and a shift toward massive private development projects.

In this specific area of Big Sky, the land change is visible over the 16-year period, with the 2005 image showing a more rural landscape with scattered development, while the 2021 image shows more expanded development, with resorts and private properties dominating the landscape. The 2011 image shows a transition period, with some development but not as extensive as in 2021.

The 2021 satellite image was captured during snowfall, which may obscure some of the land changes, but the overall trend of increased development and land consolidation is still evident.The land development highlights the increasing tourism and real estate growth in Big Sky, Montana, which has transformed the area into a popular destination for outdoor recreation and luxury living.
