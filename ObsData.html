State Overview

From 2008–2023, New Jersey shows steady urban expansion along major corridors. Land becomes more fragmented, increasing edge habitat used by geese.

Newark

Highly urbanized across all years with minimal land cover change. Geese concentrate in parks and water infrastructure.

Coast

Inland
